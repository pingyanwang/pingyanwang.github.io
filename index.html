Short Bio

Pingyan Wang is currently a PhD candidate in Computer Science at Hiroshima University, where he is working with Professor Shaoying Liu. Previously, He received an M.E. degree at Guangdong University of Technology and a B.E. degree at Zhejiang University of Technology.
